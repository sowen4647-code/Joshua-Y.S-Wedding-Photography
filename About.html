My Story

Hi i'm Joshua Finkelstein-Swartz. For over a decade, I've been blessed to document one of life's most precious moments—weddings. My approach is simple: capture authentic emotions, genuine connections, and those fleeting moments that make your day uniquely yours. I believe great wedding photography is about more than just beautiful images. It's about preserving the laughter, the joyful tears, the quiet moments between vows, and the grand celebrations with family and friends. My goal is to tell your love story in a way that feels true to who you are as a couple. Whether your wedding is in a grand cathedral or an intimate garden, I'd be honored to be part of your special day. I blend candid photojournalism with artistic portraiture to create a heartfelt visual narrative you'll treasure for generations.
